Your Lakeside Escape

Nestled by the scenic Caliraya Lake, Lake O’ Cali is a family-owned getaway offering a unique blend of nature, comfort, and adventure.

Whether you’re staying in our floating barge, glamping tents, A-frame cabin, or cabanas, you’ll find the perfect spot to relax, recharge, and make unforgettable memories. From sunrise paddles to bonfire nights, Lake O’ Cali is where simple joys meet cozy comfort.
